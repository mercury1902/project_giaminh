## Design Specifications Summary

**Aesthetic**: Minimalist + Modern Editorial + Cultural Sophistication

**Colors** (Vietnamese Five-Element System):
- Cổ đại (Ancient): `#1F2937` (Water/Black)
- Phong kiến (Feudal): `#DC2626` (Fire/Red)
- Cận đại (Modern): `#EABB00` (Earth/Gold)
- Hiện đại (Contemporary): `#16A34A` (Wood/Green)

**Typography**:
- Headings: Be Vietnam Pro (SemiBold/Bold, 24-32px)
- Body: Lora (Regular, 16px, 1.6 line-height)
- Accents: Montserrat (SemiBold, 12-14px)

**Layout**:
- Max content width: 800px
- Generous margins: 40-80px (desktop), 20-40px (mobile)
- Vertical spacing: 24px, 32px, 48px increments
- Timeline: Alternating left-right (desktop), stacked (mobile)
